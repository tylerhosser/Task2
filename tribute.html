Moe Norman

The greatest ballstriker that ever lived

Here is a list of all Moe's accomplishments:

  • 155 career Canadian Tour and other Canadian event victories.
  • 1966, 1974 - Canadian PGA Championship winner
  • 1979-1985, 1987 - Canadian PGA Seniors' Championship winner
  • 1995 - Inducted into the Canadian Golf Hall of Fame
  • 33 - Course records
  • 17 - holes-in-one
  • Several exhibition rounds shooting under 60
"I only see what I want to see. Winners see winning, Losers see losing. You are what you think you are."

-- Moe Norman
